About ComplaintBox


Students living in hostels are bound to face some basic day to day problems which need to be looked upon. The traditional method would be going to the administration and getting their complaints registered which would be a pain. This portal helps the students studying in college hostels in getting their complaints registered easily. The students only have to fill up a form which will take a maximum of 60 seconds.
